The Hunters and the Hunted

Book 1 of The Guardian Chronicles – Epic Sci-Fi Series

Book 1 of the epic sci-fi series The Guardian Chronicles – gripping, philosophical, and full of hope.

On the night of October 15th, the world changes forever.

Invisible parasitic alien entities – the Vak'tar – infiltrate human minds. They control thoughts, amplify evil, and turn humanity into their own weapon.

Dr. Sarah Chen, a neurologist, is one of the first to realize: this is not a disease – it's an invasion.

But the real battle only begins when a second alien race appears: the Anari – hunters of the Vak'tar. They don’t fight with weapons but with light, insight, and consciousness.

What if the evil within us is only amplified – and the good just as much?

Amid global collapse, a small group of resisters forms: a teacher, a priest, a veteran, a hacker – and Sarah.

They all still carry hope. And they might be the first to become guardians of a new Earth.

A novel about darkness and light, guilt and redemption, technology and consciousness.

For readers of The Expanse, Arrival, and Children of Time – yet with its own distinctive voice.
